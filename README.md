# Slouchy 🐢

A tiny Mac menu bar app that checks your posture through the camera and calls you out when you slouch.

**[Download the latest release](https://github.com/AydenS7/slouchy/releases/latest/download/Slouchy.dmg)** — free to download. Apple Silicon Macs.

Everything runs on your Mac — camera snapshots never leave your machine.

Website: the `site/` folder in this repo.
