slouchy

A tiny menu bar app that checks your camera every few minutes and calls you out when you slouch.

Download for Mac

For Apple Silicon Macs

Getting set up

  1. Open the DMG and drag Slouchy into Applications.
  2. First open: macOS says it "could not verify" Slouchy — click Done, then go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and click Open Anyway. (One time only — Slouchy is a tiny indie app, not notarized by Apple.)
  3. Slouchy appears in your menu bar and walks you through activation — then allow the camera and you're done.
  4. Sit tall. The turtle 🐢 is watching.
Everything runs on your Mac — snapshots never leave your machine.
